About Me

Hello and welcome!

My name is Serge Villa.
I was born and raised in London, UK and have a passion for teaching English, learning new languages, programming, and trading.

Teaching Experience

In 2017, I spent a year in Japan on a working-holiday visa and taught at an Eikaiwa in Tokyo called GABA. During that time, I learned how to adjust my teaching style for each student and gained a deeper appreciation of Japanese culture.

Since 2020, I have continued my teaching journey online as a tutor with Eigox.
Teaching through a computer has allowed me to diversify my lessons using videos, native articles, and interactive online activities (such as games).

My teaching philosophy is based on Stephen Krashen’s idea of Comprehensible Input — the belief that fluency develops naturally when you are immersed in meaningful language you can understand.

Strengths as a Teacher

  • 8 years of experience teaching English as a second language.
  • An amiable personality with a flexible teaching style
  • JLPT N4 level Japanese – easier to communicate and connect with students.
  • Background in Accounting – able to teach suitable Business English.
